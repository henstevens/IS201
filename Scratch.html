3 Key Steps to a Great Golf Swing

  1. Setup & Stance
    • Keep feet shoulder-width apart
    • Bend knees slightly and keep spine straight
    • Grip the club lightly but firmly
  2. Backswing
    • Rotate shoulders fully while keeping arms extended
    • Shift weight to back foot
    • Maintain a slow and controlled pace
  3. Downswing & Follow-through
    • Lead with hips and transfer weight to front foot
    • Keep eyes on the ball through impact
    • Allow your body to rotate naturally into follow-through
